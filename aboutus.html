Ijeoma Nwosu, author of Climb an orange tree, Rigo Osisi Oroma (bilingual). Ijeoma was born and lives in the United Kingdom but spent most of her formative years in the eastern part of Nigeria, West Africa. Ijeoma is passionate about passing on the igbo language to future generations. Ijeoma considers her faith to be most important to her. If she isn't spending time with friends or family, you can find her singing and strumming away on her guitar or getting her 10 thousand steps in. Climb an orange tree Rigo Osisi Oroma is her first published bilingual book.
